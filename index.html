Biography

        Junchen is a senior research engineer at Alibaba Cloud Networking Research Group, working on data center networking (cloud-native host network and cloud gateway) and mobile networking (mobile application, architecture and hardware).
        Before joining Alibaba, Junchen obtained his Ph.D. degree in Software Engineering at Tsinghua University, studying IoT systems in SUN Group under the guidance of Prof. Yuan He and Prof. Yunhao Liu, and obtained a B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology at USTC.
        Junchen has broad academic interests in the entire field of networked systems. During the past few year, he has published several papers on international conferences and journals, e.g., MobiCom, NSDI, EuroSys, INFOCOM, TMC, IMC, IPSN, ICNP, Ubicomp, IWQoS and etc., and won the best paper award of IEEE ICII 2018 and best student paper award of IEEE ICPADS 2017.
