Biography

        Junchen is a senior research engineer at Networking Research Group, XG Lab, Alibaba Damo Acadamy (lead by Hongqiang Harry Liu). Before joining Alibaba, Junchen obtained his Ph.D. degree in Software Engineering at Tsinghua University, studying IoT systems in SUN Group under the guidance of Prof. Yuan He and Prof. Yunhao Liu, and obtained a B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology at USTC.
        His academic interests include IoT Systems, Wireless and Battery-Free Sensing, Mobile & Ubiquitous Computing, and Edge Computing. Junchen has published several papers on international conferences and journals, e.g., ACM MobiCom, IEEE TMC, IEEE INFOCOM, IEEE/ACM IPSN, IEEE ICPADS, IEEE ICII, IEEE Signal Processing Magazine and etc., and won the best paper award of IEEE ICII 2018 and best student paper award of IEEE ICPADS 2017.
