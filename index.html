During undergraduate @ USTC

Kedaibiao: an interactive community for university students and their teachers

Cross-platform community Software Engineering Flask iOS MySQL Bootstrap UI/UX

Kedaibiao is our product launched for National University Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program (Entrepreneurship Training Group). I built and led a technical team of 5 people, and we developed iOS/Android/Web version of Kedaibiao. University students who joined in courses in Kedaibiao cound receive notification from their teachers, download and browse files associated with these courses, and participate in online question-and-answer.

  • (11/2015) Kedaibiao beta was released in AppStore/Wandoujia/Yingyongbao(Tecent)
  • (11/2015 - 01/2016) Kedaibiao beta was tested and run in USTC with about 300 users
  • (05/2016) With Kedaibiao, our team won the Outstanding National University Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program of USTC

    Cainiao Guoguo: a commercial application for logistics services of Alibaba

    React Native (Dynamic Loading) iBeacon Internet of Things Software Engineering iOS

    Cainiao Guoguo is a commercial application of Alibaba group for logistics services such as express tracking, online mailing appointment and etc. I took part in the development of Cainiao Guoguo as a software developer intern at Alibaba during summer vacation.     CapSim: a social network model to predict churn rate of enterprise employees

    Analytic Hierarchy Process PageRank Social Network Matlab Simulation

    CapSim is a simulator for predicting churn rate of employees in a human capital network of enterprises. The model was mainly based on AHP and revised PageRank. Moreover, factors extracted from literatures of occupational psychology were also taken into consideration in the model.

  • (02/2015) With CapSim, our team was awarded as Meritorious Winner for ICM 2015
    •

    SkyTraveller: a comprehensive application for DJI drones

    Target Tracking Image Fusion Computer Vision OpenCV Android

    SkyTraveller is designed for DJI drones, whose functions mainly includes target tracking (following specific person), virtual control handle, automatic return and panorama fusion. Target tracking uses Camshift and Kalman filter algorithms based on OpenCV for Android.

  • (02/2015) With SkyTraveller, our team won the Best Creative Award for DJI Developer Challenge and did a demo presentaion at the headquarter of DJI (Shenzhen, China)
  • (03/2016) DJI launched Phantom 4, which integrated functionalities of SkyTraveller, e.g. target tracking and panorama fusion as its main functions! To optimize performance of these functions, Phantom 4 conducted work of image processing on mainboard of the drone, instead of processing on the phone.
    •

    Playboy210: a smart domestic robot

    Face Recognition Object Recognition Gesture Recognition Voice Recognition Human-Computer Interaction iFly Kinect

    Playboy210 is a robot designed for domestic services. It can distinguish its masters and strangers through its eye, Kinect. It also can fetch small objects such as bottles for its master, by first understanding simple commands through voice recognition (iFly), then tracking targets (Kinect SDK) and finally grabbing it. The face of Playboy210, a LCD display, is used for human-computer interaction. It not only shows feedbacks of Playboy210, but also enables a wonderful home entertainment system.

  • (09/2013) With Playboy210, our team were awarded as the Runner-Up for USTC RoboGame
  • (11/2013) Playboy210 took part in "Robo into High School" program and "Homecoming Day" program
  • (05/2014) Our team with Playboy210 were interviewed by Anhui Television on special show for Chinese Youth Day
